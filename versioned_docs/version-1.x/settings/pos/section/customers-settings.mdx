## Overview

The Customers page settings control the display and functionality of the dedicated Customers management interface. This Pro feature allows you to:

- **Customer Lookup**: Quickly find and view customer information
- **Customer Management**: Edit existing customer details and information
- **Customer Creation**: Add new customers directly from the POS
- **Customer Analysis**: Review customer data and contact information
- **Advanced Filtering**: Search customers by various criteria

The settings primarily focus on customizing the data table columns to show or hide specific customer information based on your business needs.

## Columns

The Columns section controls which data points and fields are displayed for each customer in the Customers page. Each column can be toggled on or off to customize the view according to your requirements.

## Key Features

### Customer Lookup and Search

- **Quick Search**: Find customers by name, email, or other criteria
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter by customer role, registration date, or location
- **Sort Options**: Sort customers by any visible column for better organization

### Customer Management

- **Edit Customer Information**: Update customer details including contact information and addresses
- **Customer Details**: View comprehensive customer information including order history
- **Address Management**: Edit both billing and shipping addresses

### Customer Creation

- **Add New Customers**: Create new customer accounts directly from the POS
- **Complete Profiles**: Add all necessary customer information during creation

### Customer Selection

- **Customer History**: Access previous orders and purchase history
- **Contact Information**: Quick access to customer contact details

## Pro vs Free Differences

| Feature                  | Free Version     | Pro Version                        |
| ------------------------ | ---------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Edit Customer Info**   | ❌ Not available | ✅ Modify customer details         |
| **Create New Customers** | ❌ Not available | ✅ Add customers from POS          |
| **Customer History**     | ❌ Not available | ✅ View customer order history     |
| **Address Management**   | ❌ Not available | ✅ Edit billing/shipping addresses |

## Use Cases

### Retail Operations

- **Customer Service**: Quickly lookup customer information to assist with inquiries

### Restaurant and Hospitality

- **Regular Customers**: Maintain information for frequent customers
- **Delivery Orders**: Access customer addresses for delivery services

### Business Management

- **Customer Database**: Maintain comprehensive customer records
- **Contact Management**: Keep customer contact information up to date

## Customer Data Management

### Data Synchronization

- **Real-time Updates**: Customer information is synchronized across all connected devices
- **WooCommerce Integration**: Customer data is automatically synced with your WooCommerce store
- **Offline Capability**: View customer information even when offline (with periodic sync)

## Restore Default Settings

Resets all column toggles to their original default values. This is useful if the current configuration no longer meets requirements or needs a clean baseline for customer management workflows.
