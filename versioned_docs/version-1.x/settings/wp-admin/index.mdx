These settings configure the backend functionality of the POS application, including PHP and WooCommerce configurations. You can access them by navigating to `WP Admin > POS > Settings`.

## Settings at a glance {#settings-at-a-glance}

| Setting | What it does | Learn more |
|---|---|---|
| **General** | Default cart customer for all cashiers, POS-only products, decimal quantities, and the product barcode field. | [General Settings](./general.mdx) |
| **Checkout** | Payment gateways available at the POS and how POS orders are processed. | [Checkout Settings](./checkout.mdx) |
| **Email Notifications** | Per-email admin, customer, and cashier toggles controlling which WooCommerce emails fire for POS orders. | [Email Notifications](./email-notifications.mdx) |
| **Access** | User role and capability permissions for who can use the POS, including the dedicated Cashier role. | [Accessing the POS](./access.mdx) |
| **Sessions** | View active POS login sessions per user and device, and remotely sign devices out. | [Sessions](./sessions.mdx) |
| **Store Tax IDs** | Business tax identifiers (VAT, ABN, GSTIN, EIN, company-registry numbers) printed on receipts and invoices. | [Store Tax IDs](./store-tax-ids.mdx) |
| **Customer Tax IDs** | How customer tax IDs are stored, which plugin field they write to, and how they copy onto orders at checkout. | [Customer Tax IDs](./customer-tax-ids.mdx) |
