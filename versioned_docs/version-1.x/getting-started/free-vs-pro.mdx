WCPOS comes in two editions. The **free plugin** is everything you need to take payments at the counter, print receipts, and sync orders back to WooCommerce. **Pro** adds the management screens (Products, Orders, Customers, Reports), more payment gateways, coupons, refunds, and multi-store support.

If you mainly need a till that records sales, the free plugin is enough. If you also want to run the day-to-day of your store from the POS — manage stock, look up old orders, refund a customer, accept card payments through an integrated reader — that's what Pro is for.

## What's in the free plugin {#whats-in-the-free-plugin}

The free plugin from [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-pos/) covers the core "ring up a sale" workflow.

<FeatureGrid>
  <FeatureCard icon="cash-register" title="Cash + Card checkout">
    Two built-in payment methods: **Cash** with change calculation, and **Card** for when you take payment on an external terminal and just need to record the sale.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="cart-shopping" title="Cart and product search">
    Search the product panel, scan barcodes, add line items, apply line-item discounts, and add open-priced "Misc" items not in your catalogue.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="receipt" title="Receipts and thermal printing">
    Choose from a built-in [template gallery](/templates/customise) — receipts, invoices, quotes, packing slips, gift receipts, kitchen tickets — and print to 58 mm or 80 mm thermal printers over network, Bluetooth, or USB.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="sync" title="Offline storage + sync">
    Products and orders are stored locally so the till stays fast and works through a flaky connection. Orders sync back to WooCommerce when the network returns.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="barcode" title="Barcode support">
    Scan products straight into the cart with a USB or Bluetooth scanner, or use the device camera.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="file-invoice" title="Customer Tax IDs">
    Built-in Tax ID field on the customer record — VAT, ABN, GST, GSTIN, EIN, and other regional formats.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="globe" title="Cross-platform + multilingual">
    Browser, desktop (Windows/macOS), and mobile apps (iOS/Android, beta). Translated into the major European, Asian, and Latin American languages.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="puzzle-piece" title="Extension catalogue (view-only)">
    Browse the directory of POS extensions and integrations from settings. Installing them requires Pro.
  </FeatureCard>

:::note POS-only customer/coupon access on Free
On the free plugin you can still **select** an existing customer or **see** the Coupons catalogue at the till — you just can't add/edit customers, and you can't apply coupon codes at checkout. Those become available with Pro.
:::

## What Pro adds {#what-pro-adds}

Pro unlocks the rest of the back-office, more payment options, and per-store features. Every item below is Pro-only.

### Management screens {#management-screens}

The free plugin gives you a Product Panel for ringing up sales. Pro adds the full management surface:

| Screen | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **[Products](/products)** | Edit stock levels, prices, and cost of goods sold inline. Bulk operations across the catalogue. |
| **[Orders](/orders)** | Look up historical orders, reprint receipts, edit orders, and access the refund flow. |
| **[Customers](/customers)** | Add new customers and edit existing customer details (free users can only select from a picker at checkout). |
| **[Reports](/reports)** | End-of-day sales reports — totals by payment method, cashier, and store, suitable for cash reconciliation. |
| **[Coupons](/coupons)** | Browse the WooCommerce coupon catalogue from the till (free users see a blurred preview only). |

### Payment gateways {#payment-gateways}

The free plugin's "Card" gateway just records a sale that you took on an external terminal — there's no integration with the reader itself. Pro lets you use **any WooCommerce payment gateway** at the POS, and bundles several integrated gateways:

| Gateway | Use case |
|---|---|
| **[Stripe Terminal](/payment/custom-gateways/stripe-terminal)** | Direct integration with Stripe's S700 / WisePOS E readers. Supports MOTO (phone orders) and a simulator for testing. |
| **[SumUp Terminal](/payment/custom-gateways/sumup-terminal)** | Pair a SumUp card reader to the POS and complete card payments without leaving the till. |
| **[Vipps MobilePay](/payment/custom-gateways/vipps-mobilepay)** | Phone-based payments via QR code or push notification — Vipps in Norway, MobilePay in Denmark and Finland. |
| **[Email Invoice](/payment/custom-gateways/email-invoice)** | Send the customer a payment link by email; the order completes when they pay online. |
| **[Web Checkout](/payment/custom-gateways/web-checkout)** | Redirect the customer to your hosted checkout to complete payment online. |
| **Any other WooCommerce gateway** | Use the [Gateway Template](/payment/custom-gateways) to wrap any WooCommerce payment plugin for POS checkout. |

### Coupons, refunds, and the at-counter workflow {#coupons-refunds-and-the-at-counter-workflow}

<FeatureGrid>
  <FeatureCard icon="gift" title="Coupons at the register" variant="pro">
    Search and apply WooCommerce coupon codes at checkout. Coupons appear as removable pills in the cart, support sequential discounts, and respect all the usual rules — expiry, usage limits, min/max spend, product and category restrictions.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="arrow-rotate-right" title="Refunds at the till" variant="pro">
    Issue full or partial [refunds](/orders/refunds) from the POS — refund to the original payment method or as cash. Cashier and store are recorded on the refund for a full audit trail.
  </FeatureCard>
</FeatureGrid>

### Multi-store, stock, and branding {#multi-store-stock-and-branding}

<FeatureGrid>
  <FeatureCard icon="store" title="Multi-store locations" variant="pro">
    Create multiple [store locations](/stores), each with its own address, logo, tax rates, [tax IDs](/settings/wp-admin/store-tax-ids), and cashier assignments. Orders and reports can be filtered per store.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="box" title="Per-location inventory (ATUM)" variant="pro">
    Link Pro stores to [ATUM Multi-Inventory](/extensions/atum) locations for per-location stock, pricing, and SKUs.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="receipt" title="Per-store receipt templates" variant="pro">
    Assign different receipt and invoice [templates](/templates) to different stores. Each store has its own branding — logo, address, contact details — and template ordering.
  </FeatureCard>
  <FeatureCard icon="puzzle-piece" title="Install POS extensions" variant="pro">
    Install, activate, and update POS [extensions](/extensions) and integrations directly from the settings screen. Free users see the catalogue but the install controls are disabled.
  </FeatureCard>
</FeatureGrid>

### Support {#support}

- **Priority Discord support** — one-on-one assistance via a private channel, in addition to the public community channels.

## Pricing {#pricing}

Pro is sold per site from [wcpos.com/pro](https://wcpos.com/pro):

- **Annual licence** — $129 / year, includes updates and support for the licence term.
- **Lifetime licence** — $399 one-time, includes updates and support for the lifetime of the product.

Prices current as of May 2026; subject to change.

When a licence expires, Pro features keep working but you stop receiving updates and support. See [Installing WCPOS Pro](/getting-started/pro-license) for activation steps and licence troubleshooting.

:::tip Still deciding?
You can try the public demo at [demo.wcpos.com/pos](https://demo.wcpos.com/pos) (login `demo` / `demo`) — it runs the full Pro feature set so you can see the management screens, coupons, and refund flow before buying.
:::

## Ready to upgrade? {#ready-to-upgrade}

Head over to [Installing WCPOS Pro](/getting-started/pro-license) for download and activation steps.

- **v1.8+ Pro** — deactivate and delete the free plugin first. From v1.8 onwards, Pro is a standalone plugin that should not be installed alongside the free version.
- **Older Pro (< v1.8)** — keep the free plugin installed if your Pro version requires it.
